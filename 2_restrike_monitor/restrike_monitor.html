Restrike mechanics. The -20% trigger is measured on the underlying (ICE Brent 2nd month future), not on the 3BRL product itself. At -20% on the underlying, 3BRL would have lost ~60% intraday before the intraday reset — at which point a new base price is set and leverage recalculated from that point for the remainder of the session. Today's -13.3% on the underlying translated to -39.8% on 3BRL — no reset triggered, but a significant loss for holders purely from 3x leverage amplification.
